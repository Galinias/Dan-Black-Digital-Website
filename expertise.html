Expertise

Practical digital transformation for construction and property.

Every engagement follows the same shape — clarify the direction, deliver it on a real project, then embed the capability to keep it running. Below is where that shows up in practice.

Digital Strategy & Transformation

Most organisations don't lack digital tools — they lack a clear view of what those tools should achieve. We work with leadership and project teams to define a digital direction that's grounded in how you actually deliver, then turn it into a plan with real milestones.

Suited to organisations with digital ambition but no clear roadmap, or teams who've bought software that isn't being used properly.

Information Management & CDE

Information management sits at the heart of everything we do — CDE audits and setup, and governing how a project's data moves through its four states: work in progress, shared, published, and archived, in line with ISO 19650.

Suited to organisations without in-house Information Management capability, or projects where the CDE has become unmanageable.

Golden Thread & Building Safety

Practical readiness assessments and EIR & BEP review for Duty Holders and Principal Contractors under the Building Safety Act — getting requirements right at the source, not just present on paper.
