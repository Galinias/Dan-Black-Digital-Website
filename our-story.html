Our Story

Built on both sides of the same problem.

Most digital advice in construction comes from one discipline talking at the rest of the project. Dan Black Digital exists because that never sat right.

Who's behind it

Dan Black

Before this was a business, it was a career spent moving between sides of the same problem: sales, quantity surveying, Revit modelling, BIM management, and Golden Thread and information management delivery. That range is the point — most digital advice in construction comes from one discipline talking at the rest of the project. Dan Black Digital is built to make sense across all of them.

The goal is to make digital transformation in construction and property understandable and achievable — through practical strategy, hands-on delivery, courses and ready-to-use templates.

What we believe

Three things we won't compromise on.

Practical first

Everything is judged by whether it works on a live project — not just whether it looks good in a workshop.

Cross-disciplinary

Sales, quantity surveying, Revit, BIM management and Golden Thread work — brought together instead of siloed.

Built to be handed over

Capability stays with your team — not locked in our inbox or dependent on us sticking around.

Our mission

Make digital transformation in construction understandable and achievable — through practical strategy, expert delivery, and the tools to sustain it.
